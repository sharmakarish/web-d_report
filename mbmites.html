Established on 15th August 1951, by the Government of Rajasthan, M.B.M. Engineering College is one of the oldest engineering colleges in India. The college boasts of its high academic & technical standards. Considered as the pioneering Technical Institution of the State, it offers a gamut of courses both at PG and UG level. The college is committed to providing its students with an education that combines rigorous academic study and developing a far more ambitious, integrated and influential environment that will best serve the nation.

VISION OF INSTITUTE

“To be a leading educational institute that provides quality technical education and conductsresearch to produce knowledge-rich professionals for meeting the dynamic needs of theindustry and society”.

MISSION OF INSTITUTE

“To impart quality technical education to the students to make them globally competentengineers, contributing to the development of the nation and world at large”.
